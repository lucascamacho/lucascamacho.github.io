RESEARCH INTERESTS

I am interest on how ecology and evolution operates together to shape biodiversity in nature. Currently, I am researching how modularity and life history traits of simian primates drives the morphological evolution of the group. For that, I use a large data bank of linear measures of primate skulls, a data survey about primates behaviour and multivariate analysis. In this projects, I expect to contribute for a better understading on how ecology and modularity shapes primate cranial evolution.
